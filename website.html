About

The goal of a Community Based Agriculture is to bring farmers and producers to make a sustainable amount of green or healthy foods in a large community that have trouble going to and area that have healthy foods. By working together, community members can create a more resilient and environmentally-friendly food system that benefits both producers and consumers. Community-based agriculture also aims to strengthen social connections, support local economies, and promote food security for all members of the community..

Our plans


Growing Garderns

we plan to create spaces where members and farmers 4of the community can come together to grow vegetables, fruits, and herbs. These gardens can be located in public parks, schools, or other open spaces.


Host workshops

Offer educational opportunities for community members to learn about gardening, composting, and sustainable farming practices. These workshops can be led by local experts or invited guest speakers.


Organize a community-supported agriculture program

Connect local farmers with community members who are interested in purchasing shares of the harvest. Members can receive a weekly or monthly box of fresh produce, supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture practices.


Expected Results

We expect there to be an increased access to fresh, local, and organic produce for the community.Improved food security and resilience within the community, reducing the amount of fast food a community eats.
